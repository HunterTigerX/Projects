Kazuki Takemura

The man who saved the world from Lukav Minaev

  • Helped Emir Klamskoi to expose Japan Defence Force for stealing the top-secret United States of the New Continent weapon called MIDAS from a base in Alaska and a failed attempt to reproduce it.
  • Saved the scientists and his sister Alisa in Australia.
  • Helped Hua Lian rebels to save Kwang, son of murdered chairman Ming's son, to overthrow the current chairman of the DHZ Jie Bo Liao.
  • Saved people in Okinawa and stopped Lukav Minaev from mass producing the bomb of mass destruction MIDAS.
  • Exposed human experiments in Ravnui and all the people behind the MIDAS incident.
MIDAS was certainly lost in the explosion at Ocean City. We must end this madness here. We must not allow the tragedy to repeat. We have a duty to those who survived that tragedy.

-- Kazuki Takemura
